Aloo Paratha Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Knead the dough using wheat flour and water.
  2. Mash the boiled potatoes and mix with onion, spices, and coriander.
  3. Make small balls of dough and stuff with the potato mixture.
  4. Roll out the stuffed dough into a flat paratha.
  5. Cook on a hot pan with ghee or oil until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with curd or pickle.

Enjoy your delicious Aloo Paratha!
