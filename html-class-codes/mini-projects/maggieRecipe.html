Maggi Recipe

Ingredients:

- 1 packet Maggi Noodles
- 1.5 cups Water
- Maggi Tastemaker Masala
- 1 tsp Oil/Butter (optional)
- Chopped Vegetables (Onion, Capsicum, Tomato, Peas - optional)

Steps to Make Maggi:

1. Heat 1.5 cups water in a pan.
2. Add Maggi noodles and let them boil for 2 minutes.
3. Add Maggi tastemaker masala.
4. Stir well and cook for another 2 minutes.
5. (Optional) Add chopped vegetables for extra taste.
6. Cook till noodles become soft and water is absorbed.
